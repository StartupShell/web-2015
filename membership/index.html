Tracks to membership.

Shell Founders

Startup Shell Founders is the membership track that has been offered since the very beginning. Interested parties apply for membership with a venture they are currently working on. Applications are reviewed and based on many factors, the entire venture team is either accepted or deferred.

Apply as a founder.

Shell Fellows

Since the Fall of 2016, Iota batch, Startup Shell has offered another track to membership - Shell Fellows. The Shell Fellows track is for designers, business catalysts, and developers who want to offer their skills to the community. Shell Fellows are required to be active in the community at least one hour per week during the semester.

Apply as a fellow.
